Rea's Bamboo Resorts

Rea's Bamboo Resort is located at National Road Barangay Calabasa from Gabaldon, Province of Nueva Ecija, Philippines. Accommodation with Wi-Fi service available. Pets are allowed in our accommodation on request. To relax and have fun we have outdoor pool (year-round) and we have private parking in our accommodation.

Situated in Gabaldon, Rea's Bamboo Resort has an outdoor swimming pool, garden, terrace, and free WiFi. Boasting room service, this property also provides guests with a barbecue. Guests can have a drink at the snack bar.
